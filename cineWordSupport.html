1. Troubleshooting Tips

Having trouble? Check out these quick fixes:

  • App won't open: Try restarting your device or reinstalling the app for a fresh start.
  • Login issues: Double-check your username and password, or reset your password using the 'Forgot Password' link.
  • Game crashes: Clear the app cache and try again. If the issue persists, reinstall the app.
  • In-app purchases missing: Ensure your purchase was successful, then contact support if the issue persists.

2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I download CineWord? Available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).
  • Is CineWord free? Yes, CineWord is free to download and play. In-app purchases are available.
  • Can I play offline? Unfortunately, an internet connection is required to play due to online features.
  • How do I report a bug? Simply contact us through the support page or use the contact form below!
